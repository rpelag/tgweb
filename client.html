Team Global Journey

Team Global Facility Solution Inc has an established history of providing top-notch facility management solutions to businesses, backed by their timeline of success and continuous advancements in the industry.

  • Historical Expansion

  • Competent Workforce

Starting up with an initial manpower of ten people, the company has already grown to adapt with the needs of its clients. In prospectus, the company grew to a forty back-office employees, and still continues to grow to cater their clients’ needs. Team Global Facility Solutions, Inc. aims to provide companies with a Better Solution.

  • 2007: TeamGlobal started with 20 Employees
  • 2010: Our company grew to 100 Employee
  • 2014: Increased to more than 300 Employees
  • 2014: Expanded the Engineering Services
  • 2017: Enter Parnership with APMC
  • 2019: We rapidly grew to 2000 employees and closed out 200 projects
  • 2019: Expanded the I.T. Services

Each department has experts ,comprising with a wide array of individuals:

  • Recruitment Specialists
  • Consultants
  • Licensed Engineers, more than a decade of experience in their respective field
  • Department Heads backed up with immersive company backgrounds for On-line Recruitment, Head Hunting Firm, I.T. Companies and CALL CENTER’s
  • Office managers which have handled Multinational Companies
